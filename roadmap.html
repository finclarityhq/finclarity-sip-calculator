Section 05

The 2025
Master Plan.

Wealth is built through discipline, not luck. Follow this institutional-grade checklist to secure your financial decade.

Step 1: The Survival Foundation

Before investing a single rupee in the market, ensure your "Defense" is ready. 2025 requires a 6-month emergency fund parked in a Liquid Fund and a ₹1 Cr Term Insurance policy. This prevents you from liquidating your wealth during a crisis.

Step 2: The Core Portfolio (SIP)

Allocate 70% of your savings to a "Step-Up SIP." Aim for a 10% annual increase in your SIP amount to match your career growth. Focus on Index Funds (Nifty 50) and Flexi-cap funds to capture India's 5-trillion-dollar journey.

Step 3: Tax Optimization

Switch your debt allocation to Arbitrage Funds or Equity Savings Funds to enjoy the **12.5% LTCG** rate instead of your personal tax slab. Review your portfolio every March to execute "Tax-Loss Harvesting."

Step 4: The 4% SWP Target

Your ultimate goal is to reach a "Corpus-to-Expense" ratio of 25x. Once reached, you can trigger an SWP, withdrawing 4-5% annually, ensuring you never have to work for money again.
