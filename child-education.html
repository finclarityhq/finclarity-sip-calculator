The Hyper-Inflation Challenge

While the CPI (Consumer Price Index) hovers around 6%, the cost of higher education—specifically in STEM and Management—is rising at an alarming 10-12% CAGR. A degree that costs ₹20 Lakhs today will likely cost over ₹1 Crore in 15 years.

This is the "Education Debt Trap". Most parents rely on Fixed Deposits to fund this goal. However, if your FD gives 7% and education costs rise at 12%, you are losing 5% of your purchasing power every single year. You are essentially saving your way into a deficit.
