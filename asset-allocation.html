Quadrant 01

DOMESTIC GROWTH (EQUITY)

Quadrant 02

The volatility dampener. Using Arbitrage and Liquid funds to provide a 6-7% post-tax return while keeping capital safe for emergencies.

Quadrant 03

GLOBAL ARCHITECTURE (US/TECH)

The currency hedge. Investing in Nasdaq 100 to benefit from USD appreciation and global tech innovation. Protects against INR depreciation.

Quadrant 04

HARD ASSETS (GOLD)

The disaster insurance. Gold acts as a counter-cyclical asset that rises when everything else falls. A mandatory 10% for every portfolio.
