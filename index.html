Understanding SIP & Compounding

SIP allows investors to invest a fixed amount every month into mutual funds. Over time, returns generated on both principal and previous gains lead to exponential wealth growth — a phenomenon known as compounding.

Why SIP is Ideal for Indian Investors

  • Encourages disciplined investing
  • Reduces market timing risk
  • Affordable for salaried individuals
  • Higher long-term return potential than FDs
  • Ideal for retirement and child education planning

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SIP safe?
SIP invests in market-linked instruments. While short-term volatility exists, long-term SIP investments are historically rewarding.

Can I stop SIP anytime?
Yes. SIPs are flexible and can be paused or stopped anytime without penalty.

Which mutual fund is best for SIP?
Equity mutual funds are ideal for long-term goals. Consult a financial advisor for personalized advice.
