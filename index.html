Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Lumpsum Investment?
A Lumpsum Investment is a one-time investment made at a single point in time. It is ideal for investors who want to invest a large amount for a long-term goal and benefit from compounding over the years.
What is a Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP)?
SWP allows investors to withdraw a fixed amount from their mutual fund investments at regular intervals. It helps in generating a steady cash flow without selling the entire investment.
