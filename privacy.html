PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: January 2026

At FinClarity HQ, accessible from finclarityhq.github.io, we prioritize the privacy of our visitors. This document outlines the types of information collected and how we use it.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect personal identification information unless voluntarily submitted via our contact forms. We use Google AdSense and Google Analytics, which may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits.

2. Google AdSense & DART Cookies

Google is a third-party vendor on our site. It uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet.

3. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access to any data we may hold.
